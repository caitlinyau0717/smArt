Welcome to smArt

What We Believe

We began this project as part of an Urban Upgrade hackathon. To us, an essential upgrade to any city is an improvement on the quality of life that residents enjoy.

In particular, we think of entertainment, media, and the arts. These aspects of civilization are often overlooked as non-essential, but to us they are in fact gauges of the prosperity and peace of a community.

What We Hope

We hope that anyone who is interested in the common art-form is able to use our platform as a way to view public local and global art attractions with ease of access.

We strive to spread the reach of the creative output of each community, so as to inspire more people and their passions. In the era of global communication and growth, we hope that this will be an awesome tool to aid greatness.
